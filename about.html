Our story

From a Spark to a Blaze In 2015, Aarish Tinwala ignited a spark called Scraft Studios with a pocketful of dreams and limited resources. Through tireless dedication and a keen eye on the evolving digital landscape, that spark blossomed into a formidable flame.

A Journey of Triumph Over the span of 8 years, we've gone from a startup to a titan in the industry, steering over 100 clients to success and generating a proud tally of over 50 Crores in profitable revenue.

Scraft Studios Today Today, we stand as a beacon in the digital marketing sphere, transforming visions into victory narratives, ready to etch new success stories in the ever-evolving digital cosmos.
